Money Heist

"Money Heist" (original title: La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The series tells the story of two meticulously planned heists — one on the Royal Mint of Spain and the other on the Bank of Spain — led by the mastermind known as The Professor, as seen from the perspective of one of the robbers, Tokyo.
